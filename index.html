The history of Mexico could not be understood without art. Based on key works from each artistic period, this course allows students to learn about different facets of the history of Mexican art: pre-Hispanic, Viceroyalty, independent Mexico, and the twentieth century with its variants, as well as the importance of popular art in our country. All this from knowing the most representative works of each period, but also the institutions and socio-cultural and historical conditions that allowed them, and vice versa, thinking about the symbolic role of art in the construction of the cultural fabric of our country, both historically and today.
